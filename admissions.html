Admissions

We welcome students from diverse backgrounds and aim to provide an inclusive and supportive learning environment. Our admissions process is designed to identify and nurture the unique talents and potential of each child.

Step 1: Inquiry

Submit an inquiry through our online form or by contacting our admissions office. Our team will provide you with detailed information about the admissions process, requirements, and available programs.

Step 2: Application

Complete the application form and submit the required documents, including academic records, birth certificate, and any relevant supporting materials.

Step 3: Assessment

Prospective students may be required to attend an assessment or interview to evaluate their academic readiness and potential fit for our school.

Step 4: Enrollment

Upon successful completion of the admissions process, you will receive an offer letter and instructions for enrollment and registration.
