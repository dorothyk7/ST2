Academics

At Smart Touch School, we follow the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) set by the Kenyan Ministry of Education. Our curriculum is designed to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary for success in the 21st century.

Early Years Education

We provide a nurturing and stimulating environment for our early learners, focusing on developing their cognitive, physical, and social-emotional skills through play-based activities and interactive learning.

Primary Education

Our primary curriculum emphasizes the acquisition of foundational knowledge in core subjects such as Mathematics, English, Kiswahili, Science, and Social Studies. We also incorporate extracurricular activities to promote holistic development.

Beyond the Classroom

We believe in providing a well-rounded education that goes beyond academics. Our students have opportunities to participate in sports, arts, clubs, and community service projects to nurture their talents and develop essential life skills.
